## Absolute Value
- Let $x \in \mathbb{R}$. We define 

$$|x| = \begin{cases} x & \text{ if} x \geq 0 \\ -x & \text{ if} x < 0. \end{cases}$$

---

## Theorem
Let $x, y \in \mathbb{R}$.
- **(1)** Let $\varepsilon > 0$. Then $|x| < \varepsilon \iff −\varepsilon < x < \varepsilon$ and $|x| ≤ \varepsilon \iff −\varepsilon ≤ x ≤ \varepsilon.$  
- **(2)** $x ≤ |x|$
- **(3)** $|xy| = |x||y|$
- **(4)** $|x + y| ≤ |x| + |y|$
- **(5)** $|x| - |y| \leq |x-y|$
