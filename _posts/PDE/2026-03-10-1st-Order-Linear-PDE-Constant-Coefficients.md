## Theorem
The general solution of a follwing PDE

$$au_x + bu_y = 0$$ 

where $a, b$ are constants, not both zero, is given by 

$$u(x, y) = f(bx - ay)$$

for some function of one variable.

### Proof
1. Geometric Method
Let $v = (a, b)$. Note that 

$$au_x + bu_y = \nabla u \cdot v = D_vu = 0.$$
