## Definition
**(i)** An ***initial condition*** of the given PDE specifies the physical state at a particular time $t_0$.

**(ii)** A ***boundary condition*** of the given PDE specifies the physical state on the boundary $\partial D$ of the domain $D$ of the PDE.

- ***Dirichlet condition***: $u$ is specified on $\partial D$

- ***Newmann condition***: $\displaystyle \frac{\partial u}{\partial n}$ is specified on $\partial D$

- ***Robin condition***: $\displaystyle \frac{\partial u}{\partial n} + au$ is specified on $\partial D$ where $a = a(x, y, z ,t)$ is a given function.
