## Definition
A topology on a set $X$ is a collection $\mathcal{T} \subset 2^X$ such that

(i) $\emptyset, X \in \mathcal{T}$

(ii) $\displaystyle \bigcup_{\alpha \in A \subset \mathcal{T}} U_\alpha \in \mathcal{T}$

(iii) $\displaystyle \bigcap_{i=1}^n U_i \in \Tau$
