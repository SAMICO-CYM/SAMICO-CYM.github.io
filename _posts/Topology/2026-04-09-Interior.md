## Definition
Let $X$ be a topological space, and let $A \subset X$. The ***interior*** of $A$, denoted by $\mathrm{Int} A$ or $A^\circ$, is the union of all open sets contained in $A$. 

---

## Remark
**(i)** $A^{\circ}$ is the largest open set contained in $A$.

**(ii)** $A^{\circ} \subset A \subset \overline{A}$.
