## Definition
Let $(X, \mathcal{T})$ be a topological space. A ***subbasis*** for $\mathcal{T}$ is a collection $\mathcal{S}$ of subsets of $X$ such that 

$$\bigcap_{S \in \mathcal{S}} S = X.$$

$\mathcal{T} = \mathcal{T} _{\mathcal{S}}$.

---

## Theorem
Let $\mathcal{S}$ be a subbasis for a topology on $X$. Then $\mathcal{T} _{\mathcal{S}}$ is the smallest topology on $X$ that contains $\mathcal{S}$.
