## Definition 
Let $(X, \mathcal{T}_X)$ and $(Y, \mathcal{T}_Y)$ be topological spaces. A map $f: X \to Y$ is called an ***open map*** if $f(U) \in \mathcal{T}_Y, \forall U \in \mathcal{T}_X$.
