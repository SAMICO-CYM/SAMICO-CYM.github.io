## Definition
Let $f$ be a map on $\mathbb{R}$, and let $\\{ x_1, x_2, ... \\}$ be a bounded orbit of $f$. The orbit is ***chaotic*** if 

**(i)** $\\{ x_1, x_2, ... \\}$ is not asymptotically periodic.

**(ii)** the Lyapunov exponent $h(x1)$ is greater than zero.
