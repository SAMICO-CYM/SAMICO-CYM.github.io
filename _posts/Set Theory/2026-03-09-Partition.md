## Definition
Let $X$ be a nonempty set. Then a ***partition*** of $X$ is a collection of subsets of $X$ such that 

**(i)** $\forall A \in P, A \neq \emptyset$.

**(ii)** $\forall A, B (A \neq B) \in P, A \cap B = \emptyset$.

**(iii)** $\displaystyle \bigcup_{A \in P} A = X$.
