## Definition
Let $R$ be a relation on a set $X$. 

**(i))** If $R$ is reflexive, antisymmetric, and transitive, then $R$ is called an ***ordering*** (or a ***partial ordering***, or a ***poset***) on $X$.

**(ii)** An ordering $R$ on $X$ such that $\forall x, y \in X, xRy \vee yRx$ is called a ***total ordering*** (or a ***linear ordering***.)
