## Definition 1
Let $(X, \le)$ be a poset. A pair $(x, y)$ is called a ***cover*** if 

**(i)** $x \le y$

**(ii)** $\forall z \in X (x \le z \le y \Longrightarrow z = x \vee z = y)$.

We often say that $x$ is covered by $y$ or $y$ covers $x$, and denote it by $x \prec y$.

## Definition 2
The Hasse diagram of a poset $(X, \le)$ is a drawing of the elements and the covers of $X$ such that if $x \prec y$, then $x$ is below $y$. 

## Theorem
Let $(X, \le)$ be a finite poset, and let $x, y \in X$. Then $x \le y$ $\iff$ $\exists x_1, ..., x_n \in X$ such that 

$$x = x_1 \prec x_2 \prec ... \prec x_n = y.$$

### Proof
$\blacksquare$
