## Definition
Let $X$ be a set.

**(i)** Let $m \in \mathbb{N}$. We define an ***$m$-tuple*** of elements of $X$ to be a function 

$$\mathbf{x}: \{1, ..., m \} \to X.$$

We denote $\mathbf{x}$ by $(x_1, ..., x_m)$.

**(ii)** We define an ***$\omega$-tuple*** of elements of $X$ to be a function 

$$\mathbf{x}: \mathbb{N} \to X.$$ 
We also call such a function a ***sequence***, and denote $\mathbf{x}$ by $(x_1, x_2, ...)$ or $\{ x_n \}_{n \in \mathbb{N}}$.
