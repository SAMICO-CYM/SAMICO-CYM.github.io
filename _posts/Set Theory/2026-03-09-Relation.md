## Definition
**(i)** A ***relation*** $R$ from $A$ to $B$ is a subset of $A \times B$. We denote $(a, b) \in R$ by $aRb$.

**(ii)** Let $A$ and $B$ be sets, and let $R$ be a relation from $A$ to $B$. Then ***inverse*** $R^{-1}$ of $R$ is the relation from $B$ to $A$ such that $bR^{-1}a \iff aRb$. That is, $R^{-1} = \\{(b,a) \mid (a,b) \in R\\}$.

**(iii)** Let $R$ be a relation from $A$ to $B$. Then the ***domain*** of $R$, denoted by $\operatorname{Dom}(R)$, is the set 

$$\operatorname{Dom}(R) = \\{ a \in A \mid \exist b \in B (aRb) \\}.$$

The ***image*** of $R$, denoted by $\operatorname{Im}(R)$, is the set of all those $b \in B$ such that $aRb$ for some $a \in A$.
