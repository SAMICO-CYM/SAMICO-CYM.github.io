## Definition
The ***general linear group of degree $n$*** $GL(n, \mathbb{R})$ is the set of all invertible $n \times n$ matrices with real entries. 

$$GL(n, \mathbb{R}) = \{ A \in \mathbb{R}^{n \times n} \mid \det(A) \neq 0 \}.$$
