## Definition
The ***special linear group of degree $n$*** $SL(n, \mathbb{R})$ is the set of all invertible $n \times n$ matrices with real entries and determinant 1.

$$SL(n, \mathbb{R}) = \{ A \in \mathbb{R}^{n \times n} \mid \det(A) = 1 \}.$$
