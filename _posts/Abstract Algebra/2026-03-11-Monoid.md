## Definition
A ***monoid*** $(M, \ast)$ is a semigroup with an identity element. That is, $(M, \ast)$ is a binary algebraic structure such that $\ast$ is associative and there exists an identity element.

## Example

$(\mathbb{N}_0, +)$ is a monoid, but not a group.
