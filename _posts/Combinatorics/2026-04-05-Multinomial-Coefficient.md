## Definition
For positive integers $n_1, n_2, ..., n_k$ with $n = n_1 + n_2 + \cdots + n_k$, the ***multinomial coefficient*** is defined by 

$$\binom{n}{n_1, n_2, ..., n_k} = \frac{n!}{n_1! n_2! \cdots n_k!}.$$

Note that the binomial coefficients is 

$$\binom{n}{k} = \binom{n}{k, n-k}.$$
